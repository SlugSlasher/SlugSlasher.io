ABOUT

Slug Slasher is a 2D action platformer where the player takes on the role of a chef who must defend the world from an onslaught of giant slugs with only his trusty bucket of salt. Fight your way through crumbling cityscapes, the depths of the slug caverns, or even the glittering gastropod gladiator arenas of Slug Vegas—all the while smashing, dashing, and slashing your way through ridiculous enemies and crazy bosses!

The game is being made in Unity, with Tiled Map Editor used to construct levels. Development started during January of 2017 and is expected to finish during summer of 2018!

THE TEAM


Slapped Silly Studios is a three-person team of student developers from Indiana University. The group formed when Slug Slasher was pitched in Hoosier Games during the spring of 2017 and has continued development since, appearing at various Indiana University events, as well as Indy Pop Con 2017.

Having pitched the original idea for the game, Ryan Reske is currently a senior in the IU Media School's Game Design program. Additionally, he serves as the team's lead designer and social media manager and is the Vice President of Hoosier Games.
Rajin Shankar is a sophomore Computer Science student in IU's School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering. In addition to being the team's lead programmer and artist, he manages web/graphic design and project organization. He also acts as a developer and coordinator for Hoosier Games.
A sophomore Game Design student, Dakota Erickson serves as the team's lead systems designer and playtesting/QA coordinator. He is also the Treasurer of Hoosier Games.
