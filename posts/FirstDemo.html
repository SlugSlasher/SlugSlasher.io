Slash Some Slugs on Itch.io!
March 14, 2018

Have you ever wanted to become a chef that has to save the world by fighting giant gastropods with a bucket of salt? Well now you can! Slug Slasher's first public demo is available to play for free in browser over on Itch.io!

Get a taste for what's in store this summer as you make your way through the first section of game's the first level. We'll be updating this demo as time goes on, so be sure to check back for new features, new areas, and more ridiculous slugs!

Be sure to follow us on our Steam page and Twitter as well if you want to be notified of any new updates to the demo, or just stay up to date with everything we're doing!
