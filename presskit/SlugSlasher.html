Description

Slug Slasher is a 2D action platformer where the player takes on the role of a chef who must defend the world from an onslaught of giant slugs with only his trusty bucket of salt. Featuring vibrant visuals, satisfying and punchy sound design, and classic side-scrolling platformer beat-em-up gameplay, the game is sure to charm even the saltiest souls both young and old.

History

How it Happened

Slug Slasher started out as a project within Indiana University's Hoosier Games game development club. Development has continued for about a year and is due to finish during the summer of 2018.

Features

  • Classic punchy 2D action platformer gameplay enjoyable by veterans and newcomers alike
  • Over-the-top characters, environments, and situations packed with personality and filled to the brim with fun, engaging mechanics
  • Satisfying sounds supplementing your slug slashing soirées

About Slapped Silly Studios

Boilerplate
Slapped Silly Studios is a three-person student game dev team featuring programmer/artist Rajin Shankar, systems/level designer Dakota Erickson, and designer/producer Ryan Reske. The trio's first project, Slug Slasher, is poised for a summer 2018 release.
