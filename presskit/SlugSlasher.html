Description

Slug Slasher is a 2D action platformer where the player takes on the role of a chef who must defend the world from an onslaught of giant slugs with only his trusty bucket of salt. Fight your way through crumbling cityscapes, the depths of the slug caverns, or even the glittering gastropod gladiator arenas of Slug Vegas—all the while smashing, dashing, and slashing your way through ridiculous enemies and crazy bosses!

With vibrant visuals, satisfying and punchy sound design, and classic side-scrolling platformer beat-em-up gameplay, Slug Slasher is sure to charm even the saltiest of souls both young and old—so salt up your boots and get slashin' in this slime-packed adventure!

History

How it Happened

Slug Slasher started out as a project within Indiana University's Hoosier Games game development club. Development has continued for about a year and is due to finish during the summer of 2018.

Features

  • Classic punchy 2D action platformer gameplay enjoyable by veterans and newcomers alike
  • Fast-paced combat with a variety of different attacks and combos, air dashes, enemy juggling, and more
  • Over-the-top characters and environments that keep things spicy with new challenges and mechanics
  • Dozens of hidden collectible hats that can add a touch of personal style to your adventure
  • Satisfying slashing, slapping, and squishing sounds to supplement your slug slashing soirées

About Slapped Silly Studios

Boilerplate
Slapped Silly Studios is a three-person student game dev team featuring programmer/artist Rajin Shankar, systems/level designer Dakota Erickson, and designer/producer Ryan Reske. The trio's first project, Slug Slasher, is poised for a summer 2018 release.
