Description

Slapped Silly Studios is a three-person student game dev team featuring programmer/artist Rajin Shankar, systems/level designer Dakota Erickson, and designer/producer Ryan Reske. The trio's first project, Slug Slasher, is poised for a summer 2018 release.

History

From Rags to Slightly Fancier Rags

Slapped Silly Games started out as a three-person team in Indiana University's student-run game development club, Hoosier Games, beginning work on Slug Slasher in the spring of 2017. The group has continued to develop the game alongside their work as full-time students and are preparing for a summer 2018 release.

Since its creation, the team has also enlisted the help of Michigan-based game/media composer Will Savino, who is writing and producing the Slug Slasher Original Game Soundtrack.
